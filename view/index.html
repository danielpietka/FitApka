About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a value derived from the mass (weight) and height of a person. The BMI is defined as the body mass divided by the square of the body height, and is expressed in units of kg/m2, resulting from mass in kilograms and height in metres.

Calculator BMI

I wrote a BMI Calculator, so you can check a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

Remember

BMI shows the approximate body fat content. For some people, BMI may lead to wrong conclusions. Moreover, it is not recommended to use the BMI to determine body weight for children up to 14 years of age and for pregnant women.
